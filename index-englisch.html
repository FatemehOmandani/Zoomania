What's New?

Animals from Around the World in Our Museum!

Visitors can now experience fascinating animals from the most remote corners of the Earth up close. Discover new interactive stations specially designed for young researchers.

From Mini Insects to Giants: Animal Sizes in Comparison

Learn about the differences in size among animals – from tiny ants to gigantic elephants – and how they adapt skillfully to their environment.

Workshops and Activities in the Museum
